About Thrift Ahoy

Thrift Ahoy is an e-commerce platform created for sustainability, safety and saving purposes to allow students to sell and buy goods within their college communities. Using University IDs and emails, it guarantees the safety and the connection between students who sell and buy in a rapid and economic manner.
